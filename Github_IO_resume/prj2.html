Battery RUL Prediction

  • "RUL" stands for Remaining Useful Life. I used 3 types of Supervised Learning Regression algorithms, Linear Regression, Polynomial Regression and Regularization Regression on the "Battery Remaining Useful Life (RUL)" dataset with features such as Max Discharge Voltage, Min Discharge Voltage and Charging Time etc, to predict the Battery RUL and compared their accuracies, after proper data pre-processing.
